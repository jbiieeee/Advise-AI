About the System

Virtual Academic Advising Platform

Virtual Academic Advising & Enrollment Assistance System

A comprehensive digital solution designed specifically for Higher Education Institutions (HEIs) in Manila to modernize academic advising and enrollment processes.

Our integrated platform addresses the challenges of traditional, disjointed advising methods that rely on walk-ins, emails, and phone calls - resulting in long waiting times, slow responses, and unequal access to services.

The Challenge

Academic advising and enrollment processes in Manila's universities remain disjointed and inefficient, relying heavily on walk-ins, emails, and phone calls.

This traditional approach results in:

  • Long waiting times for students seeking advising support
  • Slow response times from overwhelmed advisors
  • Unequal access to services, especially for students who commute or work while studying
  • Lack of visibility into system performance and resource needs

System Objectives

Modernizing academic infrastructure by transitioning from fragmented, manual processes to an integrated digital ecosystem

Centralize Academic Access

Unified portal serving as single entry point for all advising tasks

Reduce Administrative Latency

AI chatbot handles routine inquiries, decreasing response time

Enable Remote Participation

Remove physical presence requirement for working students and commuters

Empower Data-Driven Management

Real-time analytics for proactive resource allocation
